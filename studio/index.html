Linganore Technologies

A studio for shipping useful software fast.

Not an agency. Not a single-product startup. Closer to a workshop — where one person, leveraged by AI, can take a product from idea to operating SaaS, then move to the next while the last keeps running.

The thesis

Most valuable business software is unglamorous — it replaces a manual document-and-decision workflow with something structured and auditable. There are thousands of these workflows, each too small for a big platform to bother with and too painful for the people stuck doing them by hand. AI changes the economics: a small team can now build, ship, and operate software that used to require a whole company. So instead of betting everything on one product, Linganore builds a portfolio of focused ones.

The operating model

Built around AI-assisted development. Agentic coding tools, structured-JSON data contracts, reusable document-extraction pipelines, and evaluation harnesses let one person move at a pace that used to need a team. Each product reuses the studio's shared infrastructure, so the second is cheaper than the first and the third is cheaper than the second — all grounded in public, industry-standard frameworks and open regulations.
